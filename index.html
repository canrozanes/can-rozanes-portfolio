Bio

I am a Web Developer based in Toronto, Canada and I’m a recent graduate of HackerYou College of Technology.

I have a background in Electrical Engineering and in my previous life I was a data analyst and a manager of operations at Bell Canada. There, I found my love for coding by automating parts of my job, tackling business problems with code and getting a kick out of it. I figured i needed to further explore this interest so I took a bunch of online courses followed by HackerYou’s Immersive Web Development Bootcamp.

When I’m not coding, I love reading historical fiction and non-fiction, taking my bike out for a ride, travelling and enjoying the outdoors.
